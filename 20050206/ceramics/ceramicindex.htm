BLANKS - Blanks are fired pieces, usually porcelain and mainly in 1/12th scale. Check the individual pages to learn about the clay and the scale. They are completely fired and ready to be finished. They can be painted with acrylics and then sealed with a clear acrylic spray, or if you have a kiln they can of course be finished with under and over glazes, etc. The porcelain pieces are all fired to Cone 6.

FINISHED PIECES - These are just as they sound - finished! Some are done with acrylics and some with fired glazes - it just depends on what John wants to do at the time he is doing them Most of the pieces will be one of a kind, because they are individually done.

Since all prices are affected by materials charges and exchange rates, they are subject to change without notice.

We offer wholesale terms on the blanks, but not on the finished pieces. Please contact John to ask about wholesale.
