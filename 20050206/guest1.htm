THANK YOU FOR VISITING THE SWALLOWHILL SITE

As you will see, this is not a Guestbook in the normal sense of the word, but instead it is an invitation to join our list to receive the Swallowhill Newsletter.

If you would like to be on our mailing list - we don't post very often so you won't get a ton of mail - please fill out the form below. 

When you hit the SUBSCRIBE button, the information will come to us and you will be added to an automatic list. You can, of course, unsubscribe at any time.

I had this whole thing broken down into categories for a while, like 'dolls' 'patterns' and so on. I was going to do three lists, but then found it was an awful lot of extra work. So this one list will cover new dolls, new molds, new ceramic pieces, new patterns, and usually a monthly special that is only for subscribers. I'll also try to send you some tips which I have figured out over the years. 

Many years ago - from about 1978 to about 1989 - I used to send out a monthly newsletter. In it I told about things that were happening here, including family things, and I sent out prototype patterns, specials offers, ideas and tips for people to try. Many of you have said that you have kept the newsletters and wonder if I will ever start them up again. Well......  I can do it via email! The problem was that over the years the postage got so high that I just could not charge people what it would cost me to send them a 'personal' type letter.
