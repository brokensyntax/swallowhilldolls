Have you ever wanted to make your own dolls, but don't want to go to the expense of purchasing a kiln and all that entails? 

What about polymer clay? What about the liquid air drying slip?

Have a look here, and click on the link at the bottom of the page!
http://www.swallowhilldolls.com/johnmolds/dolls.htm

You may be very pleasantly surprised to find what can be done with these materials.

All our molds are made for Production Use. This means that we use the best possible grade of plaster and a low ratio of water to plaster. The molds are generally heavier and larger than other commercial molds so that they can be poured more often.

DOLLS: While we list a few doll molds made by reducing antique dolls to 1/12th scale, the main focus is on original dolls. Sculpting new dolls is an ongoing thing here at Swallowhill and we will be adding a variety of molds to this site at regular intervals.

ACCESSORIES: The accessory molds have been added by popular demand, and this side of the business seems to be growing fast! They include such things as... dishes... plant pots... garden statuary... and so on...
