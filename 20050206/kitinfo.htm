KITS FOR MAKING DOLLHOUSE DOLLS

Doll kits are chosen to cover a good range of ages and characters in 1/12th scale. 1/12th scale means that one inch equals one foot. So if a lady is 5-1/2 feet tall, a doll in 1/12th scale will be 5-1/2 inches tall.

Every doll is hand made and hand painted with care by Joy, here at Swallowhill. The china paint is fired many times between applications so that the features will have beautiful definition.

We do our best to give you the very finest miniature porcelain dolls available. You can choose from kits made from commercially available molds, or from kits made from 
 our own Swallowhill molds.
As we add new dolls we are dropping some of the older ones. However we will still fill orders for 'old' dolls if given sufficient time to make them from scratch.

Kits mostly consist of: head, shoulderplate, hands and unpainted feet, to be made up on a flexible padded body with wire armatures,
Body patterns and directions are included - really not difficult to do!

We use felt for the body, pipe cleaners for armatures, and cotton batting for stuffing. We do not include the body pack, nor wigging materials. A body pack is available for $1.00 when ordering the kits.

 See Our "Wigging" page for information on wig kits, wig making instructions and wigging materials. 

To help you complete your project
We offer Sewing and Knitting Patterns that Work!
