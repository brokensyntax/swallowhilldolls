ABOUT THE SMALLER DOLLS:

I have always loved the really small dolls and the challenge they present, both in making the porcelain parts and in assembling and dressing them.  My small dolls at the moment run from 1" tall to 3" tall.

For the past year or so I have been concentrating on dressing these tiny dolls. Some can be used as 1/24th scale people, but a lot of them are most suitable for "dolls' dolls".

I have been collecting molds for small dolls, and John has been making some new 'tiny' molds for me. At present I seem to have enough that I can be happy for months to come. 

John is also making some 1/24th scale people molds for me. I want to have some 1/2 scale dolls with character, rather than just the dolls which can be made from commercial molds.

Let me know please if you have any special wishes, either for the dressed 'tinies' or for the dressed 1/12th scale and 1/24th scale people.
